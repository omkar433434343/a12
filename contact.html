Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. You'll also receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in original condition with all tags attached. Please visit our Returns page for detailed information.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, standard shipping is $5.99.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) and overnight shipping options are also available at checkout.

Can I change or cancel my order?

You can change or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, please contact customer service for assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay for secure checkout.
